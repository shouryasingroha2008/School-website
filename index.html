our story

Mount Carmel School (A Christian Minority Institution) is a Senior Secondary School being run under the aegis of Mount Carmel Educational Society, Chandigarh (Regd.), a group of Christian teachers, dedicated to the cause of complete moral, intellectual, Spiritual and social development of boys and girls. We derive our inspiration from the Lord Jesus Christ who loves children and we fully believe in the commandment given by Him, ‘Love thy neighbour as thyself’. On this, the motto of our School ‘Amor Vincit Omnia’ is based. Mount Carmel School was founded by Dr. Earnest Charles J. Samuel & Dr. Annie Charles Samuel in April 1987

Campus life

We believe that in order to grow and reach their full potential, our students need to develop all aspects of their personality, social relationships, physical fitness, creativity, altruism just as much as their academic knowledge. Mount Carmel's Campus offers to our students all the amenities useful for a full and interesting student’s life. The preschool classes are fun and colorful, sports field and game amenities cater to all tastes, our labs and library offer a lot of opportunities for different activities and workshops, as well as a great atmosphere for self-study for our older students. We have facilities for Recreational Activities, Huge Play Areas, Sports Equipment and Sessions, Art & Culture Activities, Olympiads, Camps, Voluntary Activities, Charity Programs and Class Rooms (Yes! Off course, very airy and alive classrooms)

Mission & Vision

Mission

Nurturing students to develop them morally, intellectually, spiritually and socially, equipping them to become self-supporting in an honourable and decent way of life so that they can be good, healthy and progressive citizens.



Vision

To provide Educational Excellence to Boys & Girls irrespective of caste, creed or colour with special preference to those from Christian minority community.
